Classic Pork Sinigang

This traditional Filipino sour soup is made with pork, tamarind, and a variety of vegetables. It's a comforting and tangy dish perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g pork ribs or belly, cut into pieces
  • 1 packet tamarind soup base or 6-8 pieces fresh tamarind, mashed
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish, sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bunch kangkong (water spinach) or bok choy
  • 10-12 string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • Salt and fish sauce to taste
  • 6 cups water

Method

  1. In a pot, bring water to a boil and add pork, onion, and tomatoes. Simmer for 30-40 minutes until pork is tender.
  2. Add the tamarind soup base or fresh tamarind juice, stirring well to mix.
  3. Add the radish, eggplant, and string beans. Let simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and fish sauce to taste.
  5. Add the green chilies and kangkong. Simmer for another 2 mi
