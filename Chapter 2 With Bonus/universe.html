The Star Wars Universe

Explore the many worlds across the galaxy, both from the Canon and Legends timelines.

Canon Planets

  • Tatooine - Desert homeworld of Anakin and Luke Skywalker.
  • Hoth - Icy planet, home to the Rebel Alliance base.
  • Endor - Forest Moon, home of the Ewoks.
  • Coruscant - Galactic capital, city-planet.
  • Naboo - Lush planet and home to Padmé Amidala and Emperor Palpatine.
  • Kashyyyk - Forest planet, home of the Wookiees.
  • Mustafar - Volcanic world where Anakin becomes Darth Vader.
  • Dathomir - Homeworld of the Nightsisters and Darth Maul.
  • Ahch-To - Remote world where Luke Skywalker exiles himself.
  • Jakku - Desert world where Rey grew up.
  • Crait - Salt-covered mining planet, site of the Resistance stand-off.
  • Exegol - Sith homeworld, hidden planet of the Final Order.

Legends Planets (Expanded Universe)

  • Korriban (now Canon as Moraband) - Ancient Sith burial world.
  • Zonama Sekot - Living planet capable of creating sentient ships.
  • Csilla - Icy homeworld of the Chiss Ascendancy (Grand Admiral Thrawn).
  • Rakata Prime - Ancient Rakatan Infinite Empire world.
  • Mygeeto - Icy banking clan planet, seen in Legends and briefly in Revenge of the Sith.
  • Malachor V - Sith world devastated by the Mass Shadow Generator during the Old Republic era.
  • Rhen Var - Icy planet rich in Jedi and Sith history.
  • Manaan - Water world home to the Selkath and producers of kolto (healing substance).
  • Telos IV - Site of a massive Restoration Project after the Jedi Civil War.
  • Dagobah - Yoda's exile world (Canon, but deeply explored in Legends).
